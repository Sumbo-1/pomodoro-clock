What is Pomodoro?

Pomodoro is a technique with purpose to improve productivity mesured by a timer. Work or study time is getting split into smaller chanks with breakes taken in between. For example, you can have 4 work sessions of 25 minutes each with 5 minutes of break taken after every session. Let's get productive!
